🫀 CPR Guide
For unconscious victim not breathing — act immediately

Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation — Step by Step

1. Lay the victim flat on a firm, level surface
2. Place the heel of your hand on the centre of the chest
3. Push down hard — at least 5–6 cm depth
4. Rate: 100–120 compressions per minute (Staying Alive beat)
5. After every 30 compressions → give 2 rescue breaths
6. Tilt head back, lift chin, seal their mouth and blow for 1 second
7. Continue cycles until emergency services arrive or person recovers

⚠️ What NOT to do in accident situations

  • Do not give food or water to an unconscious person
  • Do not remove a helmet from a road accident victim
  • Do not move a person with suspected spinal injury
  • Do not leave the victim alone — stay until help arrives
  • Do not apply a tourniquet unless bleeding is life-threatening
